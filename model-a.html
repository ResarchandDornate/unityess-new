Right-sized for the building.

Model A delivers 261–522 kWh of liquid-cooled LFP storage in a compact cabinet (1100×1320×2560 mm) — engineered for commercial rooftops, factories, campuses and data centres needing dependable peak shaving and backup.

  • 125 kW AC rated power, 400 V nominal
  • Liquid cooling for stable thermal performance
  • DC round-trip efficiency > 94%
  • 3-tiered BMS with cell-to-system protection
Built to last

8,000 cycles,engineered in India.

High-reliability 314 Ah LFP cells deliver an 8,000-cycle lifetime with multi-stage fire safety — temperature and smoke detection, aerosol and flooding suppression — rated from −30 to 55°C.
