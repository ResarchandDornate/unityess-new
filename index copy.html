Why storage

You do not wait for the grid to decide when you have power.

Storage is what changes that. Energy captured when it exists — solar at noon, wind at 3am — held, and released the moment you need it.

The Problem

Solar at noon. Demand at seven. Storage connects them.

India’s renewable capacity is real and growing fast. But solar power at noon does not help a factory running the evening shift. Wind at 3am does not power a hospital at 8pm. The grid needs something in between.

12pm

Solar at peak. Nobody using it.

Rooftop and utility solar at full capacity. We capture what would otherwise be lost.

3am

Wind runs. The city sleeps.

Generation highest at night. Storage holds it until the shift changes, the machines start.

7pm

Peak demand. Everything draws.

Factories. Hospitals. Families. We release what we stored, instantly, reliably, every time.
