Why the collection exists

One family,three promises.

Primary

Always With You.

From a 5 kWh home unit to a 6.25 MWh utility container, every system in the collection exists to answer one question the same way: what happens the moment the grid can’t? Reserve, Meridian, and Perpetual are three answers to that same question — because you matter, whatever scale you operate at.

Secondary

Proven Before Promised.

Every spec on this page — the 8,000 cycles, the <20ms switchover, the 20-year monitored lifespan — is drawn from systems already running, not systems we’re hoping will run. India’s most trusted storage company earns that by never leading with a promise it hasn’t already kept somewhere else first.

Tertiary

Empowering You.

A 3-Tiered BMS and AI-enabled EMS run underneath all three systems — the same intelligence layer, whether it’s managing a single home battery or coordinating a 6.25 MWh utility asset. Scale changes. The intelligence doesn’t.
