Utility & grid scale

Curtailed solar,turned into savings.

Model C packs 3.34–5.016 MWh into a standard 20-ft container (6058×2436×2896 mm) at 2.5 MVA — purpose-built for grid support, frequency regulation and large solar firming, and stackable in parallel to GWh-class deployments.

  • 2.5 MVA power conversion, 690 V nominal
  • Liquid cooling with C4 corrosion rating
  • Frequency regulation & renewable firming
  • Modular — stack in parallel to GWh scale
Engineered for reliability

Built in agiga-scale facility.

Every Model C is manufactured to IEC 62619 and UL 9540(A) with multi-stage fire safety — temperature and smoke detection, aerosol, flooding, plus alarm bell and strobe — rated from −30 to 55°C.
