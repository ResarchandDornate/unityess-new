About Equinox

A hybrid platform for sites that generate their own power

Equinox is built for sites that already have — or are planning — on-site solar generation, and need storage that makes that generation actually usable around the clock. It sits between Meridian's commercial scale and Perpetual's utility scale, sized for industrial sites running Open Access power arrangements.

Solar, grid and storage in one intelligent platform: solar when it's available, grid when it's required, storage always balancing the two — so the timing of generation stops being the thing that decides whether the lights stay on.
